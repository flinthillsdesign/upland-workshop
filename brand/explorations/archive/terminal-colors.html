Color Wheel Logic
Red = Website (the separate one, feels "hot"/different)
Blue = Scheduler (already its brand color)
Amber = Inquiry Hub (incoming, attention)
Purple = ODIN (intelligence, AI mystique)
Teal = Luca & Claire (creative, fresh, between blue and green)
Green = Workshop (the brand umbrella, #A5BC39 home)
