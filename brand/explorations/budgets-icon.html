Budgets — Icon Explorations

Parametric, formula-driven budgeting: a tree of line items priced by formulas over named variables, built from reusable assembly blocks. Family conventions: dark gradient tile (#161A22 → #0A0D12), #A5BC39 green accent, white shapes at varying opacity. Shown at 128 / 52 / 32 / 16px.

The thing that sets Budgets apart: prices are formulas. ƒ(x) → a value. Pure-glyph, may be weak when tiny.
D · Cascade
A variable cascading through scope into line items that sum upward. Motion of the model.

Safe, money-grounded read: a budget building up from blocks. Less distinct from a generic finance app.
