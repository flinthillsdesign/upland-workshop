The technology leaped ahead — the trust didn't.

You can't earn trust on the hard thing until you've earned it on the easy thing.

Don't launch with the most impressive feature. Launch with the most verifiable one.

Let people confirm it's right about easy things before you ask them to trust it on hard things.
