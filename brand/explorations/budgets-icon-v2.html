Budgets — Tree & Cascade Riffs

Pulling in the app's real visual signature: the colored left-spine bars marking nesting depth, indented line-item rows, linked nodes, and formulas. Single green accent per icon. Shown at 128 / 52 / 32 / 16px.

Tree
1 · Spine + rows
The signature move: a green nesting spine with indented line-item rows branching off it. Most "this is literally the app."
2 · Node tree
Cleaner abstract tree — root branching to nested nodes, green = the linked node. More logo-like, less literal.
3 · Nested rows
Closest to the UI: stacked rows indenting inward, each with a colored edge bar; green = the linked assembly.
Cascade
4 · Variable cascade
A green variable fanning down through scope into line items. Captures values flowing through the tree.
5 · Roll-up to total
Indented items summing down past a rule to a green total bar. The budget rolling up — with an ƒ nod to formulas.
6 · Tree → total (hybrid)
Both ideas at once: a node tree whose branches cascade down and roll into one green total bar.
